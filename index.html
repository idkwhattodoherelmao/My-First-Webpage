Places I definitely want to visit

I've always been fond of the cold, and ever since I could remember, I've wanted to visit places with snowy landscapes—experience skiing, snowball fights with friends, and overall just playing with snow. Of course, since The Philippines does not experience snow, I've always been envious of seeing others enjoying it on videos. I may be too old once I get the chance to visit such places, but nevertheless, I hope that I still get the chance to.

Hokkaido, Japan.
Although it may be basic, I've always been fascinated by Japan itself for its natural beauty and rich culture. Hokkaido, in particular, has always been on my mind as a dream place to visit. Its beautiful snowy landscape, famous for its incredible powder snow and diverse culinary scene, caught my eye at a very young age and has never left since then.
    
Switzerland.
The breathtaking scenery alone is enough to make anyone want to visit Switzerland. The rich culture and history, along with iconic Alpine Landscapes like the Matterhorn and Jungfrau, makes it even more appealing! Coupled with the fact that Switzerland is also renowned for its delicious chocolates and cheeses, it'd be strange for it to not be on anyone's bucket list!
    

Places I definitely do not want to visit.

This may contradict my former statement of wanting to travel around the world, but honestly I cannot tolerate extreme heat, nor do I wanna go anywhere near where snakes live. I have a nagging fear of snakes, worms, roaches, and the like. Childish maybe, but isn't it human nature to fear?

The Desert
Whether it be the Sahara, or any desert in the world, I absolutely do not wanna go there! Let alone the extreme temperatures, the mere thought of snakes , or other dangerous creatures like scorpions hidden within the sand just makes me shudder..
    
Jungles and Rainforests.
It gives me chills just thinking of dense vegetation and the creatures that inhabit them. The thought of being lost in a jungle, surrounded by unknown dangers, is just terrifying to me!
